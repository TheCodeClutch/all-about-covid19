Actions to protect yourself

Do's & Dont's

Rural
Urban

#StayHome

Use bins to discard spoiled tissues

When coughing or sneezing, discard the spoiled tissue immediately into a closed bin.

Wash your hands frequently

Your hands should be washed frequently with soap and water or an alcohol-based hand-rub. Avoid touching your face.

Cover mouth while sneezing

One should always take care, whenever sneezing or coughing, to cover your nose and mouth with flexed elbow, tissues or napkins.

#StaySafe

Social Distancing

Maintain distance. Stay a metre away from anyone who's coughing, sneezing or has fever.

Seek medical care

Seek immediate medical advice if you have fever, cough or experiencing breathing difficulties.

Wear a mask

If you have cough, fever, and difficulty in breathing, wear a mask correctly and seek medical advice.
