What are the sources and how is the data gathered for this project?

We are using the offical twitter handles of Government of India to provide news infomration, and for numbers on the website we are gathering data from this source. We want to thank these guys for making it open source and for helping all of us during this pandemic.

Who are you and what do you aim by building this project?

We, Nikhil Singh and Shreya Maheshwari, are student developers, pursuing Bachelors of Technology from Vellore Institute of Technology, Vellore, Tamil Nadu, India. We aim to give all of you a helping hand in this time of crisis, so that we could fight against it and could also prevent the further spread of the virus. We hope to provide knowledge to all so that people can make informed decisions. That's our response to this crisis.
